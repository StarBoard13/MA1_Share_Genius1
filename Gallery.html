How to do tricks:

But before going over tricks you should be able to ski pretty well. If you cannot you should watch this video:

Tail grab:

  1. Find a small jump and approach the jump in a stable speed that you are comfortable with.
  2. As you reach the lip of the jump pop off of the jump (extend your legs to get more air).
  3. Once in the air pull your knees up into your chest to make your skis easier to grab.
  4. Reach down with your hand and grab the tail of your ski, hold it until you are about to land.
  5. Let go of the skis and extend your legs into a stable position ready to land.

360:

  1. Pick a medium sized jump and approach it with speed and your knees slightly bent facing forward.
  2. As you reach the lip of the jump rotate your upper body in the direction you are spinning (Leading with your shoulders).
  3. Right before takeoff pop and swing your arms in the direction you are spinning to help gain momentum
  4. tuck in your legs a little to help maintain balance.
  5. As you are coming around spot your landing and maintain balance in the air.
  6. As you finsih the 360 prepare to land and extend your legs.

Backflip:

  1. Find a large jump with a steep takeoff for more height. Go at a controlled speed.
  2. As you approach the jump stay confident and slightly bend your knees.
  3. As you reach the lip pop hard and throw your head and shoulders back initiating the flip.
  4. Bring your knees into your chest and tuck to rotate faster, as you come around spot your landing.
  5. Extend your legs to prepare for landing and ski away in control.

Grind a rail:

  1. Start with a low, wide, and small rail.
  2. Gain a little speed and keep your focus on entering the rail.
  3. As you reach the lip slightly pop and try to land onthe rail with both skis parallel and centered over it.
  4. Keep your weight centered and keep your knees bended to help balance on the rail.
  5. As you rach the end slightly pop off it and rotate slightly back to a normal position skis parallel to the slope.
